Our Story

At Indbritz Consultancy, we offer a wide range of services tailored to the needs of nursing professionals seeking to register and work abroad. We specialize in guiding and supporting nurses through international registration processes, enabling them to explore global career opportunities. Our services encompass comprehensive assistance for registration procedures with nursing councils and regulatory bodies, including NMC UK, New Zealand Nursing Council, and Australian Nursing Council(AHPRA). We also offer dedicated support for the CGFNS verification process and ANMAC Skill Assessment. Our ultimate goal is to streamline the registration journey, facilitating nurses in pursuing their careers with utmost ease.

Indbritz Consultancy stands as a beacon of excellence with a team passionately dedicated to empowering nurses on a global scale. We take pride in serving as the catalyst that propels your career to unprecedented heights. Our forte lies in offering expert guidance and comprehensive support throughout the intricate registration processes with esteemed nursing councils and regulatory bodies.

Our team of experts guides you through each step of the registration process, handling all the paperwork and communication with the respective agencies. We strive to make the process as straightforward and stress-free as possible, allowing you to focus on your career goals. With Indbritz Consultancy by your side, navigating the registration processes becomes a breeze, no matter which council or regulatory body you're dealing with.
